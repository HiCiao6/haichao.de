Biography

  • I am currently an Asscociate Professor with the School of Aeronautics and Astronautics, Shanghai Jiao Tong Unversity.
  • I graduated from Xiamen No.1 High School in 2009 and went to the Beijing Institute of Technology with a direct Bachelor to PhD track in Aircraft Design and Engineering.
  • I earned my Bachelor's degree in 2013 and my PhD in 2019. My PhD supervisor is Prof. Dr.-Ing. Shengjing Tang.
  • My first encounter with the Institute of Flight System Dynamics, Technische Universität München (TUM-FSD), was in 2013, during my time as a visiting Bachelor's student.
  • I revisited TUM-FSD as a visiting PhD student between 2014 and 2016, mentored by Prof. Dr.-Ing. Florian Holzapfel.
  • Following my doctoral studies, I worked as a postdoctoral researcher at TUM-FSD between 2019 and 2022, guided by Prof. Dr.-Ing. Florian Holzapfel and Prof. Dr.-Ing. Dr. h.c. Gottfried Sachs.
