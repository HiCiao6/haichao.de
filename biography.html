Biography

  • I am currently an Asscociate Professor with the School of Aeronautics and Astronautics, Shanghai Jiao Tong Unversity.
  • I graduated from Xiamen No.1 High School in 2009 and went to the Beijing Institute of Technology with a direct Bachelor to PhD track in Aircraft Design and Engineering.
  • I received my Bachelor and my PhD from the Beijing Institute of Technology in 2013 and 2019, respectively. My PhD supervisor is Prof. Dr.-Ing. Shengjing Tang.
  • I got to know the Institute of Flight System Dynamics, Technische Universität München (TUM-FSD), in 2013 when I was there as a visiting Bachelor student.
  • I visited the TUM-FSD again as a visiting PhD student between 2014 and 2016, supervised by Prof. Dr.-Ing. Florian Holzapfel.
  • I worked as a postdoctoral researcher at the TUM-FSD between 2019 and 2022, supervised by Prof. Dr.-Ing. Florian Holzapfel and Prof. Dr.-Ing. Dr. h.c. Gottfried Sachs.
