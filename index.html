Research Interests

Our passion for aerospace engineering is backed by flight mechanics, mathematical rigor, and bio-inspiration! Our research focuses on safe and efficient flight mission accomplishment and the GNC problems associated with it. We develop aerial systems and techniques that enable autonomous guidance and control, enhance safety, and promote electrification for flight vehicles, realizing the transition from inspiration to operation.

  • Flight Mechanics
  • Trajectory Optimization
  • Bio-inspired Flight Techniques
  • Guidance, Navigation, and Control
  • Electric Aircraft
